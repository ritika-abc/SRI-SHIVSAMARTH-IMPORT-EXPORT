About Sri Shivsamarth Import Export – Trusted Rice Exporters from India

Sri Shivsamarth Import Export is a distinguished name in the global rice trade, known for its commitment to quality, integrity, and customer satisfaction. Founded by seasoned industry professionals Aman Condra and Manish Raut, the company leverages over 6 years of in-depth experience in the rice industry to offer superior-grade rice products tailored to meet both domestic and international market demands.

Our journey began with a clear vision — to bridge the gap between traditional agricultural excellence and modern-day consumer expectations. What started as a modest venture has now grown into a trusted business that serves clients across both B2B (business-to-business) and B2C (business-to-consumer) segments. Our strong foundation is built on knowledge, innovation, and a customer-first approach, making us a reliable partner for wholesalers, retailers, distributors, and end consumers alike. At Sri Shivsamarth Import Export, quality is non-negotiable. Every grain of rice we offer undergoes rigorous quality checks to ensure it meets the highest standards of purity, taste, and nutritional value. Our sourcing processes are ethical and transparent, and we work closely with farmers and suppliers to maintain consistency in supply and quality.

Our strength lies not just in our product, but also in our flexible and tailored packaging solutions. We offer rice in convenient packs of 5 kg, 10 kg, and 25 kg, catering to various customer needs — from individual households to large-scale commercial buyers. Each pack is designed for freshness, durability, and easy storage, ensuring that our rice reaches customers in its best condition.

Our Infrastructure – Where Quality Meets Scale

At Sri Shivsamarth Import Export, our robust infrastructure is the backbone of our operations and a key driver of our consistent product quality. We operate with a fully equipped facility designed to meet both domestic and international rice trade standards.

Key Features of Our Infrastructure:
  • State-of-the-art rice processing units with automated grading, polishing, and packaging machinery
  • Hygienic storage warehouses with temperature and moisture control
  • Bulk handling capabilities for seamless B2B operations
  • In-house quality control lab for testing purity, grain length, moisture, and aroma
  • Efficient logistics and supply chain management for timely domestic and export shipments
  • Scalable production setup to handle small retail orders and large wholesale consignments
